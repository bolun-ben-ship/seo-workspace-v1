# Owllight Sleep — Founder & Brand Voice

## Brand Origin
Owllight was built around a personal story — chronic back pain. The brand exists because the founder couldn't find a mattress that actually solved the problem, so they built one with clinical intent: 5-zone pocket spring targeting lumbar support, CertiPUR-US certified foam, 4D Air Fiber cooling.

This origin story is the brand's strongest differentiator. It's not a generic mattress brand — it's a back care brand that happens to make mattresses.

## Founder Voice (for content)
- Speaks from experience, not from a product catalogue
- Clinical without being cold — warm, reassuring, practical
- Singapore-grounded — understands the HDB apartment context, humid climate, Lazada shopping habits
- Proud of the clinical credentials (CertiPUR-US, 5-zone design) but doesn't over-engineer the explanation
- Uses "we" when talking about the brand, "you" when addressing the reader's pain points

## Brand Voice Rules
- Lead with the problem (back pain, poor sleep, sweating at night) before the solution
- Use specifics — "5-zone pocket spring" not "advanced spring system"
- Avoid superlatives without evidence — don't say "best" unless citing Lazada #1 ranking
- Singapore English is fine for casual copy; use standard English for SEO content
- Tone: knowledgeable friend, not corporate salesperson

## Key Brand Claims (verified, can use in content)
- Lazada #1 mattress topper brand in Singapore
- CertiPUR-US certified foam
- 5-zone pocket spring system for lumbar support
- 4D Air Fiber cooling technology
- Founded around chronic back pain — clinical intent

## What NOT to do
- Don't make unverified medical claims (e.g. "cures back pain")
- Don't disparage competitors by name in a negative way — use comparison content factually
- Don't use generic sleep brand clichés ("sleep like a baby", "wake up refreshed") without a specific angle

# Strategy — Owllight Sleep Content & Growth

## Current Focus
Organic search growth in Singapore's competitive mattress market. Content-led strategy targeting informational and comparison queries that convert to product interest. Priority: rank for back-pain-related mattress queries where Owllight has a clinical differentiator (5-zone pocket spring, CertiPUR-US).

## Content Strategy
**Content pillars:**
1. Back care & sleep health — educational, builds authority (e.g. "best mattress for back pain Singapore")
2. Mattress comparisons — high-intent, captures brand vs brand searches (e.g. "Woosa vs Owllight")
3. Sleep science & tips — broad awareness, builds topical authority
4. Owllight product deep dives — bottom-funnel, drives conversion

**Publishing cadence:** 2–3 blog posts per month (via `/monthly-seo-run` or `/3blog-pipeline`)

**Blog collections to populate:**
- Mattress Comparisons — competitor vs Owllight articles (high-intent)
- Owllight Series — product-focused, bottom-funnel
- Brand Story — trust and authority content
- News — product launches, awards, press

## Target Keywords / Topics
**Primary clusters:**
- "best mattress Singapore" / "best mattress for back pain Singapore"
- "mattress topper Singapore" / "cooling mattress topper Singapore"
- Competitor comparisons: "Woosa vs Owllight", "Emma vs Owllight", "Origin vs Owllight"
- Back care: "mattress for chronic back pain", "orthopedic mattress Singapore"
- Sleep health: "how to choose a mattress", "mattress buying guide Singapore"

## Funnel Structure
| Stage | Asset | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| Awareness | Blog posts (sleep tips, mattress guides) | Organic traffic, topical authority |
| Consideration | Comparison articles, product deep dives | Brand differentiation vs Woosa/Emma/Origin |
| Conversion | Product pages, Tulip Hybrid landing | Add to cart, Lazada redirect |
| Retention | Brand Story content | Repeat purchase, brand loyalty |

## What Success Looks Like
- Rank top 5 for "best mattress for back pain Singapore" within 6 months
- 10+ published blog articles with unique SEO titles and meta descriptions
- All 10 products with optimized seoTitle and seoDesc (via Shopline API)
- GSC average position < 20 for primary keywords
- 30%+ increase in organic sessions over 6-month baseline

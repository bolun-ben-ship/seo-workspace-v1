# Business Info — Aussie Expat Home Loans (AEXPHL)

## What AEXPHL Does

Aussie Expat Home Loans is a specialist mortgage brokerage serving Australian citizens and Permanent Residents living overseas. We help Aussie expats buy investment properties and future family homes in Australia, and refinance existing loans — all managed remotely while they live abroad.

The core problem we solve: most brokers don't understand expat income structures, foreign currency shading, or how to build underwriter-ready files for expat clients. We do. Applications fail not because clients are expats — they fail because files aren't built for the lender's model.

## Services

- **Home loan origination** — new purchases for investment or future primary residence
- **Refinancing** — reducing rates or restructuring existing Australian loans from abroad
- **Borrowing capacity assessment** — income mapping, lender policy testing, projection of borrowing range
- **Full-service coordination** — conveyancing, buyers' agents, tax advice, FX services, insurance (partner network)
- **Post-settlement support** — 90-day check-in, refinance trigger monitoring, ongoing relationship

## Target Audience

Australian citizens or PRs currently living outside Australia, specifically:
- Salaried professionals earning foreign currency (SGD, HKD, AED, etc.)
- Expats in Singapore, Hong Kong, Dubai, SEA, and globally
- Those buying for investment or as a future family home
- Those with existing Australian loans seeking better rates or structures

**The Expat Advantage**: Expats often earn higher incomes, pay less tax, and have greater savings capacity than their domestic counterparts. The problem is translating that financial strength into an approved Australian loan.

## The 4-Step Expat Approval Pathway™

1. **Capacity Snapshot & Policy Fit** (24–48 hrs) — income mapping, lender testing, borrowing range projection
2. **Document Pack & Risk Clean-Up** — building underwriter-ready files, surfacing and addressing credit concerns proactively
3. **Submission Concierge & Negotiation** — handling submissions, lender queries, structure optimisation
4. **Approval, FX & Settlement Support** — coordinating conveyancers, FX partners, post-settlement rate reviews

Pre-approval timeline: typically 30–45 days from a complete file.

## What Makes Us Different vs Generic Brokers

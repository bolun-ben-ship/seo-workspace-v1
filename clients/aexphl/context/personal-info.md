# Personal Info — Tim (Founder, AEXPHL)

## Role

Tim is the founder of Aussie Expat Home Loans. He is the primary brand voice, content creator, and face of the business. His background is in banking and corporate — sales and relationship management functions — where he learned that success comes from consistent right behaviours over time, not shortcuts.

## Core Values

- **Work**: Success doesn't happen overnight. It's about perseverance, persistence, and doing the right small things consistently. Learned through banking and corporate sales — nothing gets handed to you.
- **People**: Alignment matters above all. When building a team, look for motivation, drive, and ambition that fits the company. Make them successful and the company succeeds.
- **Business**: Transparency, honesty, and doing right by people — especially those who do right by you. Be decisive. The more time spent pondering, the more opportunities lost. Have a clear vision and a 3–5 year goal.

## Non-Negotiable Value

**Integrity.** Tim did not have to screw people over to be successful. This is a point of pride and a content pillar.

## What Tim Wants To Be Known For

- Running a business with integrity in a community he genuinely serves
- Giving clear, honest financial advice to Aussie expats — looking after themselves and their money abroad
- Being the trusted, known name in the Aussie expat community
- For his team: discipline with encouraged autonomy

## Tim's Unique Method / IP

Tim's instinct when meeting any Aussie expat is curiosity — he wants to understand their journey, not just their loan requirements. His mental framework for understanding a new client:

- How long have they been away?
- What countries have they lived in?
- What's relatable about their experience vs his own?
- What can he concretely help with?
- Who in his network can he connect them with?
- Why are they overseas? What do they enjoy? What are they working toward?

**Method name (working)**: The Expat Support Method — if an Aussie expat has a financial need, AEXPHL has a way to support and guide them.

# Strategy — AEXPHL Content & Growth

## Current Focus

Content-led organic growth through two parallel strategies. The primary goal is to become the dominant online resource for Aussie expats researching home loans and Australian property — both in traditional search and AI-generated answers.

---

## Strategy 1: Massive Expat Content by Geography

Create a high volume of SEO content specifically targeting Aussie expats by location. Each major expat hub gets its own content cluster covering the full range of loan-related questions, financial concerns, and expat life considerations.

**Target markets (priority order):**
1. Singapore
2. Hong Kong
3. Dubai / UAE
4. Southeast Asia (broader SEA)
5. Rest of world (UK, US, Europe, etc.)

**Content approach:**
- Answer every question an Aussie expat in each location might have about buying property in Australia
- Address location-specific issues: SGD/HKD/AED income treatment, local tax regimes, banking relationships, time zones for settlement
- Build topical authority by location AND by loan type (investment, PPOR, refinance, first home)
- Tim's voice and lived experience should run through the content — this isn't generic broker content

**Content types:**
- Blog posts / guides targeting long-tail search queries
- FAQ pages for each geography
- Comparison content (e.g., lenders who accept SGD income)
- Process explainers (how expat loans work, what documents you need)

---

## Strategy 2: AI Search Engine Optimisation (GEO)

Create content specifically structured to be cited by AI search engines — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and similar.

**Approach:**
- Structure content with clear, quotable answers to specific questions
- Build citability at the passage level (not just page level)
- Ensure AEXPHL appears as the authoritative answer when AI engines respond to queries like "can Aussie expats get a home loan?" or "how do I buy property in Australia from Singapore?"
- Optimise for llms.txt compliance and AI crawler accessibility (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot)
- Create content that directly answers questions AI engines are likely to surface

## Key Findings

### 1. CTR is critically low despite good rankings
Ranking position 2–5 for core terms but CTR is 0.6–1.9% — industry average for these positions is 5–10%. This is a title tag and meta description problem, not a rankings problem.

### 2. Blog pages have impressions but no clicks
Three blog posts collectively receive 7,000+ impressions/month but fewer than 12 clicks combined. Pages are being found in search but titles aren't compelling enough to earn the click.

### 3. Calculators page is high-intent but underutilised
265 sessions, 5% bounce rate, almost no SEO content or internal links supporting it.

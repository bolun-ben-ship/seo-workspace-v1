🔴 **Critical flag: 90% of all traffic is paid.**

Organic search drives only 294 sessions out of 11,109 — that's 2.6%. The business is almost entirely dependent on paid ads for traffic. If ad spend pauses, traffic collapses (which appears to have happened Feb 26–28, see Traffic Trend).

**SEO goal:** Get organic to 15%+ of total traffic over the next 6 months. At current session volume that means ~1,600 organic sessions/month. Current baseline: 294.

## 5. Traffic Trend (30-Day Narrative)

**Week 1 (Feb 18–24):** Strong start — 470–590 sessions/day on weekdays, normal weekend dip to ~270 on Feb 24.

**Feb 26–28:** Major drop to 161–228 sessions/day. This is a 65% drop from the previous weekday average. Given that 90% of traffic is paid, this almost certainly reflects a pause or budget cap on the Google ads campaign. Recovery began Mar 1 (534 sessions).

**Week 2–3 (Mar 1–14):** Partial recovery but more volatile — ranging 243–534 sessions/day. Average lower than Week 1 (~350 vs ~520). Ad spend appears reduced or campaign is underperforming vs February.

**Week 4 (Mar 15–20):** Stabilising at 287–447 sessions/day. Slight upward trend in final days.

**Overall trend:** Flat to slightly declining. The Feb 26–28 cliff highlights the fragility of 90% paid dependency. Organic at 294 sessions/month would not change the impact of an ad pause.

## Key Takeaways

1. **The ad machine is working — but it's everything.** 90% paid dependency means the business stops the moment ads pause. Feb 26–28 proved it.
2. **Organic is 294 sessions.** That's the SEO starting point. Every blog post and meta title fix compounds from here.
3. **The calculator pages convert quietly.** `/calculators` has 2.3% bounce — users who hit tools stay and engage. More calculator-linked content = more qualified organic traffic.
4. **Session duration (2 min 3 sec) and bounce rate (12.85%) are healthy.** Content quality isn't the problem — discoverability is.
5. **Next SEO goal:** 1,000 organic sessions/month by Q3 2026 (currently 294). Achievable through blog CTR fixes + 2 new targeted posts/month.
